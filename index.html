About Me

Hi, my name is Logan. I'm a mechanical engineer in robotics and product development. These days I've been at a fast-pased startup in the energy industry developing water reclamation technology to reduce water usage in power plants by 20%. I love this kind of work because I get to build something new every day, and often it's something that I've never seen until the day before.

If you've found your way here, don't be afraid to reach out and say hello. I'm always looking for new projects!
